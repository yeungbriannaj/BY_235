Fluffy Pancakes: Made in 15 minutes


Ingredients

  • 2 cups / 300g plain / all purpose flour
  • 4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 cup / 55g white sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1 3/4 cups / 435 ml cups milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract or essence
  • 4 tsp butter

    • Instructions

  • Place flour, baking powder, sugar and salt in a bowl, whisk to combine.
  • Add egg, milk and vanilla. Whisk until lump free, no longer than 30 seconds.
  • Use medium heat. Add 1/2 tsp butter and swirl to melt.
  • Pour 1/4 cup batter into the middle of the fry pan.
  • Swirl pan lightly to spread or use the ice cream scoop edge to spread into 11cm / 4.5â€³ circle
  • When bubbles rise to the surface, flip and cook the other side until golden.
